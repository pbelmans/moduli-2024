Location

The conference takes place on the Belval campus of the University of Luxembourg. If we organize your accommodation, you will stay on campus.

Public transport (in second class) is free within Luxembourg.

Lectures take place in Maison du Savoir, in room 4.510 (on the 4th floor).

Food options

For lunch you can choose between

  • the university restaurants (one can only pay cash!); for a quick lunch there is an option on the first floor of the building the lecture take place, but more extensive lunch options exist;
  • any of the restaurants in Belval: a quick search will show the options.
